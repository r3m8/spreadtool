# Stock Spread Calculator

Free stock spread calculator. Compare your stock transaction execution price with real-time Yahoo Finance market data. Compatible with Trade Republic and other brokers.

This site allows investors to analyze the execution quality of their stock transactions by calculating the spread between the price paid and the market price at the time of the transaction.

## Key Features

- Spread calculation by ISIN with Yahoo Finance historical data
- Fee and commission analysis
- Calculation history with average spreads
- Multilingual support (French and English)
- Simple and fast interface
